Higher Purpose Academy is a community dedicated to helping people discover, honor, and pursue their life purpose through profound and meaningful experiences.

Emily Lily and Kelly James are professional Life Coaches with over 20 years of combined teaching, coaching, and leadership experience. They believe everyone is meant to align with their Higher Purpose and live an abundant life. Through mindfully designed retreats and workshops, they deliver a curated collection of tools and techniques based on their eduction through the Martha Beck Institute, Byron Katie's School for The Work, and The Chopra Center; as well as extensive studies in emotional intelligence, the mind-body-spirit connection, and energy work.
